Privacy Policy

TradeClock - Global Stock Exchange Hours

Introduction

TradeClock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application TradeClock (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

TradeClock is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial information or trading data
  • Contact lists or social media information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access

Information We DO Collect

App Preferences: The App stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Selected stock exchanges you want to display
  • Custom order of exchanges in your list
  • App settings and configurations

Important: All preference data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms and is never transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Information

The locally stored preferences are used solely to:

  • Remember your selected stock exchanges
  • Maintain your preferred order of exchanges
  • Provide a personalized user experience
  • Ensure app functionality across app sessions

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • No internet connection is required for the app to function
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

TradeClock does NOT integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates completely offline and independently.

Children's Privacy

TradeClock does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in financial market information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data retention policy. Local preferences are stored on your device until you:

  • Clear the app's data manually
  • Uninstall the application
  • Reset your device

Your Rights

Since TradeClock does not collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply. However, you can:

  • Clear all app data through your device's app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Reset your preferences within the app

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Google Play Store requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) guidelines
  • Android app privacy standards

Consent

By downloading and using TradeClock, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Note: This privacy policy reflects the current functionality of TradeClock as an offline application that does not collect personal data. If future versions include online features or data collection, this policy will be updated accordingly.
